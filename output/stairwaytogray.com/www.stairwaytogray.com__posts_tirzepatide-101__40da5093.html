You should never feel entitled to a vendor refunding, replacing orders, or providing the typical customer service you may feel entitled to in the regulated market you’re used to. Finding a Vendor⌗

Vendors⌗

The Vendor Promos channel contain promos from minimally vetted vendors with a track record of delivering product and are currently known not to be scammers. Familiarize yourself with these and check out the Sources and Testing channel for further questions. You may want to consider sticking with a vendor who offers Alibaba Pay, PayPal or other payment options for your very first purchase since this is the easiest payment method to use if you’re unfamiliar with the other payment methods such as crypto.

Group Buys⌗

For your first purchase check out Vendor Promotions. Sometimes, vendors call their promotions “group buys”, so vendor “promos” are synonymous with “vendor GB”. Please do not join non-vendor group buys as a newbie. This type of GB is organized by an individual; they’re a scam magnet and tend to end in many people losing their money, so it’s best to avoid trying to discern which one is “safe” to join until you’re more comfortable in the community. We also have no sway with individually organized GBs, so cannot step in and mediate if something goes wrong for you. Sticking with the vendors in Vendor Promos gives us a little more safety in numbers, and the Mods of STG can help mediate if you run into an issue that your sales rep is not resolving with you.

International vs Domestic Warehouses⌗

Pretty much all gray API tirzepatide is manufactured in China. When ordering from a vendor and shipping from China, expect a risk of U.S. Customs seizure for international shipments unless you specifically ask for domestic warehouse shipping. Not all vendors offer shipping from a US domestic warehouse. Tirzepatide is not a DEA-scheduled drug, but there is a risk of having the U.S. Customs Agency seize and report your package for wrongful labeling or just calling it HGH even when it’s not. So there’s a non-0% risk of having Global Entry and Security Clearance repercussions when accepting international shipments of tirzepatide.

When You’re Ready to Order⌗

Check the Vendor Promos channel to see how to order from your chosen vendor’s latest promotion, and contact the vendor salesperson using the contact method they provide. If using Alibaba Pay, the vendor will send you an Alibaba order “contract” with the dollar amount; ignore the order description as this will be a “stealth” order description. Use the same email you provided your sales rep as the email you made an Alibaba account with. Never contact your sales rep thru Alibaba. We do not reocmmend using crypto for you first purchse unless you are already familiar with using crypto due to the risk of losing your money if you process the payment wrong. If you still choose to do so, please do your due dilligence on understanding how to use crypto; read through the Crypto 101 guide.

Shipping⌗

We typically expect orders to take 2-8 weeks to arrive in the gray market. This is not Amazon where package tracking and customer service are always available. Check your vendor’s shipping guarantee if they have one. Some may reship after an amount of time, some will not state a concrete number and you will need to reach out to your sales rep after several weeks. Many times shipping tracking numbers are incorrect, or will not show up in the USPS system until they clear Customs and are scanned into the system. Patience and adjusting your consumer expectations is key here.

Returns⌗

There are no returns in gray market. If the vendor made a mistake and shipped the wrong product, it is advised you not reship your package to any address they provide you. If a vendor asks you to reship the product, you should not feel obligated to correct their mistake and reship for them. Reach out to an STG Mod if this happens to you and we can help mediate the issue. If you need a reship due to a purity guarantee, the vendor may ask you to destroy the product which is fairly common and okay to do so.

Testing and Quality Control⌗

Testing Your Order⌗

It’s very highly recommended that you test every order! Joining a testing group to split costs, or testing individually provides insights into the product purity and proper vial fill (mass) for the tested peptide. Sometimes vendors mislabel or ship low quality product. The primary goal with testing is to ensure you’re receiving the peptide you ordered, at a high quality, and with the expected amount of API tirzepatide.

Group Testing⌗

Pool vials from the same batch and split testing costs with others for thorough batch checks. Testing gives you peace of mind that you’re using actual tirzepatide and at the right dose. Join a Group Test by visiting the Group Test Invites channel and seeing if any groups have formed for your vendor and batch. Ask questions in the Sources & Testing channel.

3P vs. 2P Vendor Tests⌗

We recommend always testing a vial (or more if group testing) from your batch. Vendor tests (2P) are an okay start when considering which vendor to choose, but 3P test results give us unbiased confirmation. If vendors decide to be biased about which test results they release, Vendor COAs are as good as toilet paper 冒聼搂禄. So we do not blindly trust 2P vendor COAs.

Does Sterility Matter?⌗

Most gray researchers do not need to test peptide vials for sterility. Filtering with sterile supplies, along with using Hospira BAC water, is good enough for mitigating bacterial growth in your reconstituted vial. You should practice the aseptic methods recommended in the Reconstitution guide below, but subcutaneous injections such as weekly injected tirzepatide are very low risk for serious infection. Those who are immunocompromised may want to take extra precautions.

What Do I Test?⌗

The primary test you’ll want to order is High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) to test for the presence of tirzepatide, its purity, and the mass fill of the vial in milligrams (mg). The most trusted labs currently for peptide testing are Janoshik and PeptideTest. You’re looking for a test result of 98%+ purity and no more than +/-15% mass fill from labeled mg. For further information and additional periodic tests to consider running, check out Testing 101.

Reconstitution⌗

What is Reconstituting?⌗

Turning your lyophilized powder tirzepatide vial into an injectable liquid that you can dose with an insulin syringe.

Shelf Life⌗

Reconstituted vials are typically recommended to be used within 28 days when stored in the refrigerator. Adopting aseptic techniques 芒聙聰 such as wiping down the vial’s rubber stopper before each use, filtering the solution, and using Hospira BAC water 芒聙聰 can help extend this time frame. The primary goal is to mitigate risk of bacterial growth. Always examine your vial carefully before each use. If the solution appears cloudy or contains gel-like particulates, discard it immediately, as these are signs of bacterial contamination or peptide aggregation 芒聙聰 both of which are unsafe for injection.

Pfizer Hospira BAC water⌗

This is one of the very rare times we will insist on an actual brand name. Stick to Pfizer Hospira for BAC water and avoid sketchy “reconstitution water” from Amazon sellers. Hospira is the gold standard for bacteriostatic water becuase of its sterility and pH quality control, and the most readily available BAC water in the U.S. Some states require a prescription for Hospira BAC and thus Amazon will not sell this. Checking pH⌗

GLP-1 peptides currently on the gray market typically reconstitute at an ideal pH balance, but it’s wise to verify pH before injecting from your first vial in a kit. Tirzepatide should fall in the 6-9 pH range, if reconstituting outside this range you risk the peptide potentially not dissolving into solution, degrading faster, or causing discomfort at the injection site. Use inexpensive pH strips from Amazon to test your reconstituted tirzepatide by applying a couple units worth to the strip. Using Hospira BAC which is already buffered to an ideal pH itself should be enough to reconstitute at an ideal pH for the GLP1s. source

How Should It Look?⌗

Reconstituted tirzepatide should completely dissolve and be completely clear. Check out our guide on What to Look for in Lyophilized Peptides to learn how to identify high-quality and poor-quality lyophilized and reconstituted peptides.

Dosing and Injections⌗

If you are not coming from prescription tirzepatide and so do not already have a dosing schedule, the following is the currently recommend dosage protocol for tirzepatide based on Phase 3 trials. source:

Month Week(s) Dose Administration Notes
1 1芒聙聯4 2.5 mg/week Initiation phase; allows the body to adjust to the medication.
2 5芒聙聯8 5 mg/week Gradual escalation to improve efficacy while monitoring tolerance.
3 9芒聙聯12 7.5 mg/week Further dose increase if tolerated; assess GI side effects.
4 13芒聙聯16 10 mg/week Standard therapeutic dose for many patients.
5 17芒聙聯20 12.5 mg/week Higher dose for those needing additional efficacy and tolerating well.
6+ 21+ 15 mg/week Maximum recommended dose; monitor for side effects and adjust as needed.

If you’re moving from prescription tirzepatide, you can continue using your dosing protocol.

Dosing Schedule Considerations⌗

  • Starting Dose: Begin at 2.5 mg or lower to minimize gastrointestinal side effects.
  • Dose Escalation: Increase every 4 weeks based on tolerance, but many take this much slower. Sometimes moving up in dose every 2 months or more after adjusting to the 2.5mg starter dose.
  • Injection Site: Subcutaneous (abdomen, thigh, or upper arm). Rotate exact injection site weekly.
  • Timing: Inject once weekly or every 7 days. Because tirzepatide has a half-life in the body of 5 days, it takes about 20-25 days for it to build up in your body for it’s maximum and steady intended level. source

Health Monitoring⌗

If you have a primary care physician you trust, work with them to develop a health panel tailored to your situation. However, be aware that licensed physicians will not condone the use of research peptides and could decline to provide a treatment plan. If that’s the case, your alternative is to take charge of your own health monitoring by conducting periodic blood-work yourself.

For individuals using peptides, it’s generally recommended to run a comprehensive health panel once or twice a year, including:

  • HbA1c: To monitor long-term blood sugar control.
  • Liver Function Tests (ALT, AST): To check for liver health and detect potential side effects.
  • Renal Function Tests (eGFR, Creatinine, BUN): To ensure kidney health.
  • Electrolytes (Sodium, Potassium): To check for imbalances that can arise from GLP-1 use.
  • Lipid Profile (Cholesterol, Triglycerides): To assess cardiovascular risk.
  • Thyroid Function Tests (TSH, Free T4): To rule out any thyroid abnormalities, as these can sometimes be affected.

Other Tips⌗

Salt vs. Base Form: Gray-market tirzepatide is pretty much entirely available in salt forms, such as tirzepatide sodium or tirzepatide acetate, whereas the FDA-approved brands Mounjaro and Zepbound and 503a/b compliant compounding pharmacies use the tirzepatide base form. Salt forms can influence properties such as pH, shelf stability, and solubility, often improving the latter. source While no peer-reviewed studies have specifically examined tirzepatide salt forms, there are no known health risks associated with using tirzepatide sodium or tirzepatide acetate, which are most commonly found in the gray market. Notably, pharmaceutical salt forms account for approximately 50% of new FDA-approved drugs, underscoring their general safety and widespread use. source However, since salt forms can potentially alter the bioavailability and stability of the active pharmaceutical ingredient (API), it’s reasonable to recognize this distinction 芒聙聰 particularly for those transitioning from 503a/b compounding pharmacies, which typically use tirzepatide base raw powder form rather than tirzepatide salt lyophilized powder forms. source

NEVER accept unsolicited DMs from anyone looking to sell you stuff; they are almost always scammers pretending to the vendor. If the ‘vendor rep’ of the community you just joined DMs you with a price list, do not reply and block them. Real vendors will not DM you first; you reach out to the vendor yourself. The same rule of thumb goes with phone calls or emails in life. Always go to the known source of contact info which, for gray, means the Vendor Promos channel in the TG group or the vendor’s server/group official post to initiate an order. Leverage the Telegram group and ask questions. In this unregulated space, it’s up to us to hold vendors and suppliers accountable 芒聙聰 never assume they have your best interests at heart.
