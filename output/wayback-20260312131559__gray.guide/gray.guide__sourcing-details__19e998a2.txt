What Stairway To Gray Actually Is
​
'STG - NO OILS' is a Telegram community strictly for people who already know what they are doing with peptides.
This is not where you go to ask "What is bacteriostatic water?" or "How do I calculate doses?" Those questions will get ignored—or worse, you'll be removed for not doing your homework.
If You Are New, You Probably Aren't Ready for Stairway To Gray
​
I know that sounds harsh, but hear me out. If you can't immediately answer the following questions, STG isn't for you yet:
The Math:
How many mg are in 0.1mL if you reconstitute 10mg of powder with 2mL of BAC water?
The COA:
What specific values should you check on a vendor's Certificate of Analysis? Do you know the difference between purity and fill rate?
The Verification:
How do you verify if the vials you received are from the same batch used to run the 3rd-party blind tests?
Lost? That's fine. Most people are when they start.

Quick FAQ
​
Can I join both?
​
Sure. Lots of people stay in Gray Guide even after joining STG to help new members.
Why does Stairway To Gray say "NO OILS"?
​
"Oils" refers to oil-based injectables, specifically anabolic steroids. STG focuses strictly on water-based peptide reconstitution. They do not discuss steroids.
How long before I'm ready for Stairway To Gray?
​
If you're actively researching and testing, probably a couple of weeks. You'll know you are ready when basic questions start to bore you and you want deeper, scientific discussions.
Is STG coming back?
​
STG has faced disruptions before and has always reconstituted. We're monitoring the situation and will update this page when they're back online. For faster updates, check the #stg-updates channel in PRG.
Bottom Line
​
STG is an incredible resource if you are experienced. However, if you are new, you will likely get frustrated and possibly make dangerous mistakes trying to keep up.
Start with Gray Guide, learn the fundamentals, and graduate to STG when you're ready. Both communities are solid—just pick the right one for where you are right now.
