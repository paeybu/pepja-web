Path-Specific Guides: Overview & Supply Comparison

🔗 Jump to your path:​

  • Path A: Ready-to-Use - Pens, pre-filled syringes, tablets (minimal preparation)
  • Path B: Authentic Vial - Branded medication, requires drawing doses
  • Path C: DIY Powder - Highest risk, requires reconstitution (HIGH RISK)

This part provides detailed procedural information for each of the three paths described in Getting Started. Navigate to the section corresponding to your chosen path. Each path requires different steps, equipment, levels of user involvement, and carries distinct risks.

Remember to keep the universal safety principles and dosing warnings from the Universal Safety Principles section in mind throughout.
