Sourcing Ancillary Supplies (BAC Water, Syringes, Swabs, Sharps Containers)
For individuals pursuing Path B (Authentic Vials) or especially Path C (DIY Powder), obtaining the necessary
sterile
ancillary supplies is critical for safety. Sourcing these properly is arguably as important as sourcing the medication/peptide itself.

Local Pharmacies
​
Many community pharmacies (e.g., CVS, Walgreens, Rite Aid) carry:
Insulin syringes and pen needles (often available OTC).
Alcohol swabs (70% isopropyl).
Sharps containers (ask the pharmacist).
When purchasing in-store, verify that all items are individually sealed and labeled “sterile.”
