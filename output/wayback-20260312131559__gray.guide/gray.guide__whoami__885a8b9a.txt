So, who's talking?
I'm the guy who looked at four-figure pharmacy quotes for GLP-1s and muttered, "Nope, there's got to be another way."
Two years, three peptides, and a mountain of needle caps later, Gray.Guide is my pay-it-forward project.
What I actually do
​
Road-test the stuff.
Semaglutide, tirzepatide, and other peptides—if it lives in the peptide family, chances are I've pinned it and logged the aftermath.
Run the clubhouse.
Our Telegram crew sits at ~250 members. Someone's always awake to walk a newcomer through dilution math or swap nausea hacks. You will not be left hanging.
Keep vendors honest.
Two suppliers have shipped clean product to me (and plenty of others) for a solid year. If your order from them blows up, I'll step in and rattle cages.
Call out the bad actors.
Separate scammers channel, bright red flags. We'd rather you learn from our bruises than earn your own.
What we
don't
do
​
We don't give medical advice, and we're not your doctors.
What we
do
share is real-world experience: what landed, what back-fired, and what we'd never try again. Double-check everything with a clinician before you copy us.
Why bother sharing?
​
Because
sharing beats gate-keeping
. After walking dozens of friends through their first research order, keeping the workflow under wraps just felt selfish.
Ground rules
​
No commissions, affiliate codes, or "special" discount links. Ever.
Pages get updated the minute new data makes them wrong. If I'm slow, yell.
Treat Gray.Guide as a flashlight, not a prescription pad.
Welcome to the gray. Let's figure this out together.
